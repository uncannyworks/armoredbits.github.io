Programmer Recruitment

One of the core business goals of Armored Bits is to create a system where programmers can shine in a gloriously competitive environment, testing their programming skills to the utmost limits while they carve their way to the top of the ranked league system. Those who choose to opt-in will potentially be contacted at the end of each ranked season in regards to employment opportunities with interested employers and an initial interview set up.

Player Programmable

Our largest technical goal is to create an environment where players can use their programming language of choice to interface with and control their robots in real time. We do not want to arbitrarily restrict people to a limited subset of programming languages!

Artificial Intelligence

Or brute force? It's entirely up to you as to how you attempt to dominate the battlefield. Ultimately we want to appeal to everyones intellect and hopefully provide them with a worthy challenge.

Multiplayer

Strictly an online multiplayer team deathmatch format. Build a clan/guild and destroy the ranked ladders with your top secret code. Use custom in-game communication protocols to coordinate with your team and track down and destroy the enemy.

Massive Robots

This is as much a game as it is a programming challenge. Watch matches in real time as your robots destroy each other in a visceral 3d format!

Learn Programming

Our final goal is to create a programming community full of tutorials, guides, open source language SDKs where even a beginner can sit down and enjoy the experience of programmatic robot battles on their very first day.
